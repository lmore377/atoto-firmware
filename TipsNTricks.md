# Just random tips that could be useful

- If your radio is missing the settings to change the boot logo, go into Settings > System Settings > Date & Time then change the date and time to 2008-01-08 01:08 24H. After that, press back twice then go to Display settings and you should be able to scroll down to Change boot logo.
- If you go into Settings > System Settings then hold your finger anywhere on the screen for 7 seconds it'll bring up a menu where you can hide some items from the Main Menu and change the brightness and contrast of the screen.
